Quicksort in JavaScript

Posted on 2015-12-01

Quicksort (sometimes called partition-exchange sort) is an efficient sorting algorithm, serving as a systematic method for placing the elements of an array in order. Developed by Tony Hoare in 1959, with his work published in 1961, it is still a commonly used algorithm for sorting. When implemented well, it can be about two or three times faster than its main competitors, merge sort and heapsort.

– Wikepedia

Quicksort is a super useful and efficient sorting algorithm. It follows the following the procedures:

  1. Get a pivot (usually the middle point) from the dataset to be sorted.
  2. For all the items smaller than pivot, move them to pivot’s left; For the bigger ones, move to right.
  3. For the left and right sets, repeat the above two steps until there is only one item in each set.
